Amazon Key

The Amazon Key is the step in the right direction as it revolutionizes the thought and idea of delivering packages right to your home. It has a camera and a monthly subscription and it ensures that the the delivery man will not steal anything as there will be a camera watching the person the whole time. Also, it comes with a new door lock which the delivery man receives a one time code and to open. Sure there is a risk but at least it a further innovation to correct the delivery system from preventing people from stealing packages from your doorstep.
