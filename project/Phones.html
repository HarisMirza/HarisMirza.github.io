Phones

Iphone X

With the release of the newest Iphone X , Apple has jammed packed their newest phone with all the newest features they could put. Retailing at $1299 for its all new phone with a all new storage capacity of 256GB, you are sure to enjoy such a user friendly mobile device with no home button this time, wireless battery charging and more. Although critics have said it is like a Samsung Galaxy phone but it quite different.

Galaxy S8

As always, Samsung never lets its customers down especially with this one which is a good rival to the Iphone X. Retailing at $725, the Galaxy S8 is cheaper than the Iphone X and it has the ability to use a pen and write things down that will actual store into the data of the phone. The Galaxy S8 is one of many consistent phones but this one is very special to them as it is a very good time to release phones and it is a very user friendly style phone.

Google Pixel 2

The first attempt at a Google Pixel was an ok phone, the Google Pixel 2 is major improvement with all of Google's best features and famous search engine implemented into it. Retailing at $649, it is cheaper than the other two phones but don't the price fool you as it is just as powerful as the other two phones. Google has done a job setting a stepping stone into putting stiff competition against the giants of the phone industry that are Apple and Google.
